Athena Studios: Storytelling Made Classic

Athena Studios & Athens

Our Story

Athena Studios is a new state-of-the-art sound stage development that will be the first of its kind in Athens, Georgia. Upon completion, the 45-acre campus will feature over 350,000 square feet of purpose-built space for film and television production. It will provide multiple sound stages, support buildings, production offices and include an all equipped educational facility.

Athena Studios is a partnership between Athens-based commercial real estate development and brokerage firm Reynolds Capital and developer Tim Burgess. Together, they bring over fifty years of commercial real estate development, construction, and brokerage experience to this project.

Why Georgia?

Since 2008, the state of Georgia has offered tax incentives for film and TV production. That, paired with Georgia’s temperate climate, has resulted in a Hollywood Boom that has been unmatched. The state’s breathtaking scenery and historic towns make it easy for producers to choose Georgia to film their next blockbuster hit.

Why Athens?

Only a one-hour drive from Atlanta, Athens is often referred to as the state’s Classic City. The city is jam-packed with action and home to a uniquely vibrant downtown that flows beautifully into the University of Georgia’s north campus. Athens is home to an array of unfilmed “picture-perfect” locations ideal for any scene. Athena Studios hopes to benefit the local economy by bringing in new revenue to many businesses that range from restaurants and hotels to retail boutiques and hardware stores.
